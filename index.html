FAQ

Common Questions

What if I'm in debt?

Most of my clients start in debt. Phase 1 of coaching is debt elimination using the snowball method. Investment comes after the debt is cleared or at least stabilized.

How much do I need to start investing?

Money Market Funds start at KES 1,000. Consistency matters more than size. I've had students build meaningful portfolios starting from KES 500/month contributions.

Do you teach forex or crypto?

No. I teach what I practice — T-bills, bonds, MMFs, SACCOs, and equity investing. Forex and crypto require different expertise that I'm not qualified to teach.

Is this only for Kenyans?

The wealth vehicles are Kenya-specific, so yes — if you're earning in KES. The Kenyan diaspora (UK, US, Canada) also benefits because they're often supporting family in Kenya and want local investment strategies.

How long until I see results?

Budget clarity happens in week 1-2. Debt reduction begins in month 1. Meaningful portfolio growth typically becomes visible 12-24 months in. Compound interest is patient work.
